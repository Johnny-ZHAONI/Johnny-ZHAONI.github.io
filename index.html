Rickshaw Boy

A small hypertext fiction based on Lao She’s novel.

The Morning

Beijing, early morning. The street is still gray. Xiangzi wakes up in a small rented room. His body is sore, but his dream is simple: one day, he will own his own rickshaw.

Today, he has a choice. He can go out and pull the rickshaw, saving every coin. Or he can stay inside, rest his tired body, and ignore the world outside for one day.

If you were Xiangzi, what would you do?

YES — Go out and pull the rickshaw NO — Stay inside and rest

This website is a small hypertext fiction for a web design project. You can move between pages by following different links and see how Xiangzi’s day changes with each choice.
